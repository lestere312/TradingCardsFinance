We currently have a page for all major tables besides intersection tables
for our M:M relationships. The Deck and Collection page are two different
ways to represent similar data.

Accounts:
Shows each acount with option to delete them and create a new one

Deck:
Shows each deck at the top of the screen with options to add cards and decks.
Selecting a deck will show you each card in the deck and the user. Cards from here
can be deleted as well as the entire deck with the delete deck botton.

Collection:
Shows all cards in all collections in a table while showing what
collection they are a part of. Lower on the screen is a table displaying
which accounts own each colleciton. There are also options to add cards
and collections.

Card Database:
This page shows all the cards that can be part of collections
and decks. Here you can search by for sale and name as well as add cards
and update them.
